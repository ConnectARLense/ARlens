AR LENS

- Developed By Arc2I developers


AR LENS is a Augmented Reality application which convert the figures into 3D models. Image beside the text is the icon of that application. To download the application click on icon of the image. After the getting the download and install successfully Open the application.


You are just 1 step away from 3D models.



User has to scan the QR code for model to see the particular figure. For example if you scan the this QR code then you see the (Sectional 3D view of brain). To access the model from all direction, just rotate the QR code. Developer generate the Entier PDF of each Model. To download the PDF you click on QR Code.


..........Now you are Good To Go............


Gallery: 3D Models of AR Lens

[ BuckyBall ]

Buckyballs are composed of carbon atoms linked to three other carbon atoms by covalent bonds. Model made for specially 10th standard student.

[ Mechanical Engine ]

It is mechanical engine model which can be useful for students and professional to explain or understand the engine. You can get 360° view in Application.

[ Neuron ]

This Model is a anatomical structure of Neuron. 360° of neurons, cell and connectivity gives better idea. Model useful for student, Doctor and in any institute.

[ Excretory System ]

The excretory system is the system of an organism's body that performs the function of excretion, the bodily process of discharging wastes. This Educational model is used to SSC, HSC and Medical Personnel.

[ Nerve Stimulation ]

The given model is of muscle contraction through Impulse(Action Potential). By this model, you can conclude that how brain signal transmits from muscle to Motor Neuron and Motor Neuron to Muscle. This Model can be useful to SSC, HSC, and Medical Personnel.

[ Respiratory system of Human ]

The given model shows a basic respiratory system of Human and its organs. By this model, SSC, HSC, and other medical personnel can learn or teach another person.

[ Power Wheel Chair ]

Power Wheelchair is a class II medical device that is used by a person who is physically disabled by lower extremities. It is also categorized as a Rehabilitation Medical Device. The basic application of this device is to provide mobility to The Physically Disabled Person.
